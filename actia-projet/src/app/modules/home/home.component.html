ACTIA ES THROUGHOUT THE YEARS

From 2005 to 2009
Our Humble Beginning

ACTIA ES was created in 2005 with 5 employees and is one of the 4 entities of the ACTIA group in Tunisia. Untill 2009 we had less than 100 employees

From 2009 to 2012
Facing Startup Battles

From 2009 to 2012, our company operated with a workforce of fewer than 200 dedicated individuals. Despite our modest size, we thrived on the strength of our team's passion, innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence. Each member played a crucial role in propelling our company forward.

From 2013 to 2018
Embracing Growth

Between 2013 and 2018, our company experienced significant growth, leading to a pivotal transition in our physical location. As our employee count steadily increased (More than 400 employees), we recognized the need for expansion to accommodate our expanding team and operations

January 2023
NOW

With our employee count surging beyond 750 and continuing to rise, we made the strategic decision to transition from leasing to owning our corporate headquarters. This shift not only signifies our growth but also reflects our commitment to long-term sustainability and investment in our future.
